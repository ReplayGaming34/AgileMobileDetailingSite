Paint Correction and Ceramic Coating

Window Tint

Every car in Houston needs tint. The summer time heat becomes unbearable and the sun continues to pound down the entire day. This is especially true if your car sits outside all day or if you have long commutes across the city.

Window tint helps keep your interior cooler, it makes your car look better (that’s always a plus), and it adds privacy from peeping eyes looking in your vehicle.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coatings are the ultimate protection for your vehicle’s paint. They provide a durable, long-lasting shield against environmental contaminants, UV rays, and minor scratches.

With a ceramic coating, your car will maintain its glossy finish and be easier to clean, as dirt and grime have a harder time adhering to the surface.
